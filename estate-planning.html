Trusts and Wills, Estate and Probate Administration

Legal representation across generations

Life is almost always better if you have a plan. At Lausier &s; Lausier LLC, in Marblehead, Massachusetts, we assist clients of all ages to protect their assets, establish legacies for their families, and retain control of their health care decisions.

We do this using the tools of estate planning, including wills, trusts,special needs trusts, guardianships, conservatorships, powers of attorney, health care directives, designations of beneficiaries, and designations of executors, trustees and managers.

Examples of situations in which our lawyers assists clients:

  • Adults with parents who are aging
  • Survivors who need assistance in settling an estate
  • Families that wish to plan for their young children
  • Protection for disabled loved ones
  • Small businesses owners who wish to protect their assets

Flexible solutions in Estate Planning and Real Estate Law

Frequently, our clients come to us with matters that involve aspects of estate planning and real estate — such as a family that needs to sell a property as part of settling an estate.
