Residential and Commercial Property

Real estate transactions require knowledgeable legal counsel and personalized representation. It also helps if the lawyer advising you in the transaction is familiar with the area and the properties involved. Local attorney Pat Lausier has those traits.

Having worked locally for more than 30 years, both as a certified public accountant and a lawyer, Pat Lausier understands the financial and legal underpinnings of real estate transactions, and how to represent her clients to achieve the most favorable outcomes.

As a longtime local resident, she is familiar with the local real estate market and, many times, the individual properties and owners involved. Through her practice at Lausier & Lausier LLC, she can protect your rights at all phases of a residential or commercial real estate transaction, from preparing and reviewing transactional documents to facilitating the closing.

Our law firm assists clients in Marblehead, Salem and elsewhere in the North Shore and Greater Boston areas, in buying and selling residential and commercial properties. We assist clients in all matters involving:

  • Purchase and sale agreements
  • Leasing
  • Title review
  • Broker agreements
  • Easement negotiations
  • Document review
  • Short sales
  • Foreclosure negotiation
  • Free Consultation

We can remove the uncertainty and fear from any real estate transaction, including those occurring as part of a probate process.

Lausier & Lausier LLC, in Marblehead, represents clients throughout the North Shore and Greater Boston areas of Massachusetts, including all of Essex County and the cities of Marblehead, Salem, Swampscott, Beverly and Lynn.
