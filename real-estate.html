Residential and Commercial Property

Real estate transactions require knowledgeable legal counsel and personalized representation. It also helps if the lawyer advising you in the transaction is familiar with the area and the properties involved.

Having worked locally for more than 25 years, both as a certified public accountant and a lawyer, Pat Lausier understands the financial and legal underpinnings of real estate transactions, and how to represent her clients to achieve the most favorable outcomes.

The Law Offices of Lausier and Lausier LLC is familiar with the local real estate market and, many times, the individual properties and owners involved. Our attorneys can protect your rights at all phases of a residential or commercial real estate transactions, from preparing and reviewing transactional documents to facilitating the closing with the leader of your choice.

The Law Offices of Lausier and Lausier LLC assists clients in Marblehead, Salem and elsewhere in the North Shore and Greater Boston areas, in buying and selling residential and commercial properties

  • Purchase and sale agreements
  • Leasing
  • Title review
  • Broker agreements
  • Easement negotiations
  • Document review
  • Short sales
  • Foreclosure negotiation
  • Free Consultation

We can remove the uncertainty and fear from any real estate transaction, including those occurring as part of a probate process.
